
A few months ago the town faced a horrific tragedy called [[The Night of Fear]], where the [[Templar Orderium]] and [[The Night Hags]] faced off in a brutal confrontation, and many were killed in the crossfire. 

Since then the town has been hurt and marred by the event, and rumblings of a dark and sinister group known only as [[The Brothers Necromina]] are starting to gain traction.
