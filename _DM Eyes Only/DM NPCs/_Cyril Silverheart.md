# Backstory

Cyril used to be a member of the [[The Bringers of Light]], an elite band of the [[The Templar Orderium]]. He settled in [[Town, Daydream]] after falling in love with his wife [[Rosie]]. The [[Templar Orderium]] deemed his service complete - after all - what do they fight for if not for love and the happiness of the Realm?

Shortly after settling in [[Town, Daydream]] they have a kid and name her [[Tulip Silverheart]] after [[Rosie|Rosie's]] mother. They spent many years happy together, opening up a Tavern called [[The Mead]] and generally enjoying their simple life together.

When [[Tulip Silverheart]] was around 9 years old, she went with her mother out into the fields to search for some new materials for her new flute. She and her mother went missing that day and [[Cyril Silverheart]] spent months in agony searching for them both before calling on [[The Templar Orderium]] to come help.

When [[The Templar Orderium]] show up, they quickly found the root cause. The [[The Night Hags]] had taken [[Tulip Silverheart]] and [[Rosie]] and had been torturing them in their dreams for months in some twisted and sick amusement. The [[Templar Orderium]] and the [[The Night Hags]] faced off in a massive battle that the town now calls [[The Night of Fear]]. Many were killed in the clash between Light and Dark, and the [[The Night Hags]] were disbanded and fled the town.

This traumatic event had radicalized **Cyril**. And he then, in secret, he vowed to put his town of [[Town, Daydream]] under control, no matter the cost. *""Light has caused as much chaos in this fight against Darkness, and the only true order I can recall was when any town was subjugated under Darkness. The Light is the chaos.""*

He forms [[The Brothers Necromina]], an evil cult with one goal; to subjugate [[Town, Daydream]] with the power of darkness, thereby controlling and bringing order to chaos.

Months after this event, his twisted plans are now set in motion. [[The Templar Orderium]] send out a Paladin [[Wyldheim]] to scout the town and make sure that [[The Night Hags]] are still not in the area. When **Cyril** found him, he sacrificed his innocent soul in a dark ritual, and carved his essence and blood into a Talisman. He conjured [[_The Horned Devil Necros]] to his service and he now has bargained with him to subjugate the town in Darkness.
