#monster

**Necros** is his true name which he hides from everyone, including his most trusted advisors. He goes by many aliases including but not limited to: **Galeer, Ilight, Prosata, and Hu-an**. These are all lower demons which he has defeated on his rise through the ranks.

His chief lieutenant [[Gathrol]] is his most powerful tool, and committed his success.
