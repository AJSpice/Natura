# Ordinary World

Setup our heroes and their world. Set the stage and make the heroes feel as if this is boring and uneventful.

# Call to Adventure

Within the ordinary world some information will prompt our heroes to leave the Ordinary World for the Special World.

### Refusal of the Call

The heroes may refuse the Call out of obligation, insecurity, or fear of the unknown. 

# Meeting the Mentor

The heroes meet a guide or protector, often a seasoned traveler. They offer our Hero's training, knowledge, confidence, advice to help the journey.

# Crossing the Threshold

The Mentor leads the Heroes to the Threshold of the adventure, but the Heroes must take the final plunge. The Threshold is often guarded and the Heroes must prove themselves to cross.

# Tests, Allies Enemies

The heroes explore the new world and go through tests and trials. The heroes should not succeed at everything, they will form bonds with each other and new friends and foes.

# Approach to the Inmost Cave

The heroes will prepare for the major challenge that faces them. 

# The Ordeal

The heroes will face their greatest challenge yet. They confront the primary Antagonist and face their deepest fears and death.

# The Reward

The heroes take possession of the reward/treasure by facing the Ordeal.

# The Road Back

The heroes are driven to complete their adventure by returning to the Normal World. Now changed from their journey. The Road Back may often be just as dangerous as the Call To Adventure.

# The Resurrection

The heroes are once again tested as they return to the Normal World. The heroes experience a final moment that's more complete than before.

# Return with the Elixir

The heroes return home with some reward/treasure that will help them improve the Normal World.
