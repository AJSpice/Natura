
# Ordinary World

Our heroes are introduced to the simple fisherman town of [[Town, Daydream]]. *"Things are simple and quaint in the town of Daydream, with people who look out for each other, children who scurry across the streets playing, and many local shops that are run by and serve the community."*

They open in the quaint tavern/inn of [[The Mead]], and are introduced to [[Cyril Silverheart]] and other NPCs.

# Call to Adventure

Our players are thrust into a nightmare by a cult of [[The Night Hags]], lead by [[The Night Hag Queen]] who has cast Rary's Telepathic Bond on all the [[The Night Hags]] in order to have the players all share the same nightmare. In their captive dream they fight [[Monsters, Skeletons|Skeletons]], [[Monsters, Imps|Imps]], and [[Monsters, Specters|Specters]], and things are far too easy. The players must escape this dream. 

As they escape they see glimpses of [[The Night Hags]] above their beds, shapes of darkness and cruelty. [[Cyril Silverheart]] and [[The Bringers of Light]] stand at the door and a bright light appears - banishing the devils. [[Cyril Silverheart|Cyril]]; With an arrow in his shoulder and blood dripping from his mouth, says softly *"Help us..."* and collapses.

### Refusal of the Call

The heroes may refuse the Call out of obligation, insecurity, or fear of the unknown.

# Meeting the Mentor

The heroes are reintroduced to [[Cyril Silverheart]], and it is revealed he used to be a prominent member of [[The Templar Orderium]], and served in their elite Squadron [[The Bringers of Light]]. As the players were in their captive dream, [[The Mead]] was attacked by [[Monsters, Skeletons|Skeletons]] and [[Monsters, Ghosts|Ghosts]]. The Paladins that are now in town are his old companions, and members of the same elite force. They are investigating the disappearance of [[Wyldheim]], a Paladin that arrived months ago from the Order but has not been seen since.

# Crossing the Threshold

The players will be lead to their first adventure in the town by [[Cyril Silverheart]] or members of [[The Brothers Necromina]]. They wish to have the players grow in strength to serve their greater purpose.

[[The Bringers of Light]] are investigating and trying to find [[The Night Hags]] and anything else at play.

# Tests, Allies Enemies

The players will work with [[Cyril Silverheart]] and the [[The Brothers Necromina]] without knowing it to defeat small bands of evil in the town. They face increasingly stronger threats.

[[The Bringers of Light]] start to uncover more of what is going on in [[Town, Daydream| Daydream]].

The players find and defeat [[The Night Hags]]

The players find and defeat [[The Night Hag Queen]]

# Approach to the Inmost Cave

The players will learn the truth behind [[Cyril Silverheart]] and the [[The Brothers Necromina]]. It is revealed that they were only pawns in his game, and only getting stronger to be sacrificed to [[The Horned Devil]].

[[The Horned Devil]] will take over and reveal himself, betraying the [[The Brothers Necromina]], as [[Cyril Silverheart]] only asked for [[Town, Daydream]] to be controlled, and he will now do it himself. 

# The Ordeal

The players will face [[The Horned Devil|Necros]].

# The Reward

The heroes take possession of the reward/treasure by facing the Ordeal.

# The Road Back

The heroes are driven to complete their adventure by returning to the Normal World. Now changed from their journey. The Road Back may often be just as dangerous as the Call To Adventure.

# The Resurrection

The heroes are once again tested as they return to the Normal World. The heroes experience a final moment that's more complete than before.

# Return with the Elixir

The heroes return home with some reward/treasure that will help them improve the Normal World.
