
Kingdoms rich in ancient grandeur, halls carved into the roots of mountains, the echoing of picks and hammers in deep mines and blazing forges, a commitment to clan and tradition, and a burning hatred of goblins and orcs - these common threads unite all **Dwarves**.

#### Short And Stout
Bold and hardy, **Dwarves** are known as skilled warriors, miners, and workers of stone and metal. Though they stand well under 5 feel tall, **Dwarves** are so
broad and compact that they can weigh as much as a human standing nearly two feet taller. Their courage and endurance are also easily a match for any of
the larger folk.
