The Children as they are also called by my people, they are what in part was saved by [[The Old Gods]] as **The Heat** arrived. Seven of their highest is what leads [[The Divine Court]], and those beneath them also serve their tasks wisely to protect what was saved.

The **Malâk** are worshiped by some cultures as gods themselves, and some reluctantly accept it, but they do know their true purpose on [[Natúra]] is to *Save*, for they and it were saved by [[The Old Gods]] sacrifice.

Many notable **Malâk** throughout the years have shaped and *Saved* as they were tasked and I will list them below as I know best to document.

---
[[Thórin]]

Said to have taken the appearance of a large [[Monster, Dragon|Dragon]], and worshiped by the [[Race, Dragonborn|Dragonborn]], as they are believed to be their ancestor. They have not been seen in many thousands of years since they fought in the [[Battle of Smoke and Flames]] which did save the [[Race, Dragonborn|Dragonborn]] and their first kingdom [[Aeléria]].

---
[[Faléa]]

The most noble messenger, **Faléa** is entrusted to travel the earth and work directly with [[The Divine Court]]. They are the one most often appearing in times of need to convey knowledge from their superiors and to *Save* through communication.
