
A separate realm from [[Natura]] filled with the most peculiar of species. Not much is known of their connection to [[Crea]]. Not often do those who inhabit the **Feywilds** cross over into [[Natura]], and even more rarely to the other effect. What is known is there exists to be many varieties of Faerie and Pixie in their world, and they do have a rich and unknown history. Of their highest kind is [[The Fey-Court]], and members of this order only speak their native tongue of [[Language, Fey|Fey]]. 

I charge myself with knowledge above all, but to [[Natura]] lies my primary obligation, thus even I do write sparsely of The Feywilds and its kind.
