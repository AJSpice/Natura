# Strongholds

### [[Stronghold, Portséa]]

The smallest of the strongholds to the most west of **Natúra**, consisting of many fishing towns. Travel and commerce are most to occur in this Stronghold and they are not to be underestimated, many of their Kingdoms are fierce and committed warriors, especially on the seas where they are most skilled.
