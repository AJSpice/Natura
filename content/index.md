# The People

[[Natúra]] is home to many kinds of those saved by [[The Old Gods]]. They're sacrifice has given us our lives and it said our traits still echo of theirs.

[[Race, Aasimar]]
	The Aasimar are Mortals who are believed to be blessed by [[The Malâk]]. They carry with them a divine spirit, and their traits reflect it.

[[Race, Dragonborn]]
	Perhaps the most ancient beings of our world, these Dragonborn are believed to be in some way directly descended or related to [[Tulkós]].

[[Race, Dwarves]]
	Stout and hardy, their resilience and strength should be envied by any who align with them, and feared by any who don't.

[[Race, Elves]]
	If not the Dragonborn, we are considered the earliest of children. I am proud to say that we are known to be the keepers of knowledge and history.

[[Race, Gnome]]
	An ingenious and creative people, they are filled with the inspiration to create. Their societies reflect the passion of [[The Old Gods]].

[[Race, Halfling]]
	The calm and the quiet Halflings are secluded and quiet. They have tight-knit homes and communities, and excel in stealth and have stout hearts.
