
Surviving in fragmented texts from many cultures, and through the incidental dispensation of knowledge from [[The Malak]], many stories have been told of **The Old Gods**. Cultures have come and gone in my time, and their knowledge has done the same, but through these texts we attempt to understand better the beginning of [[Crea]].

[[Pleo]] and I have spent many years combining the mysteries in order to present them to [[Natura]]. It is our ultimate purpose.

*“In the beginning of what was being created there were simply **Them.** **Them** created, **Them** is how [[Crea]] came to be.”* ([[Crea Begins]], A.2)

***They** were strong. And **They** were noble. But when the **Heat** had arrived, **They** could not stop it, not without the greatest of sacrifices. And we now follow, for it is of the most noble heart to pay your life to save your children* ([[Of Why We Fight]], 0B) 

Of what we know that came of their demise is sparse, indeed even [[The Malak]] remember very little. So thus began the wise assignment of [[Pleo]] to study and to know.
