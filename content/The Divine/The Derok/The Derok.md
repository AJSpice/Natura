*“They were Saved as the rest were. But long after **The Heat** They became weary. They lost knowledge and they lost mercy. As they lost grace they became conceited and villainous. And they now bicker as ungrateful children. Their powers are self-serving and they have lost what once made them worth Saving.”* ([[The Ungrateful]], C.1)

These once-great Divine Beings are now known for their anarchy and chaos inside their own. Unlike [[The Malak]] which have a structure of their ranks, **The Derok** are constantly clawing among themselves for power. Among their lower are what are called by the Common Tongue as **Demons**,
and among their higher in strength are what they call **Devils**. At all times they keep among them thirteen **Devils**, but their members are often changing. *The Thirteen Devils* are only kept in their rank by their strength, and as they are overtaken by more ambitious or powerful **Demons**, they are replaced.
